About Us


We are a primary school with grades ranging from R to 7. We have enthusiastic, well-trained teachers. We envision kids who are passionate about their country and whose ideals and contributions to the nation are relevant.
Mzamomtsha Primary School established in Western Cape, spends time training students and encouraging abilities to emerge and be of significant value in changing society for the better.
The school stood with the goal of being chosen as Honors Primary School. We train students to succeed in a globalized society. Curiosity and a desire to learn are the most valuable qualities we can inculcate in our children.



Our Vision


Our vision at Mzamomtsha Primary School is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practice the core values of the school: respect, tolerance & inclusion, and excellence.
